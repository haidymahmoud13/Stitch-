Stitch

Hi there! Name’s Stitch but if you knew me back in my wild days, you'd call me Experiment 626. I was made in a lab by a mad alien genius named Jumba, who basically cooked me up to be the ultimate chaos machine. Super strength? Got it. Super brain? Oh yeah. Cute enough to sneak cookies and get away with anything? Absolutely.

My Nicknames
Experiment 626

Before I was Stitch, I was Experiment 626 built by a mad alien scientist to be fast, strong, and super smart. I was made to be a little blue weapon, but somehow still ended up adorable. The whole galaxy knew me as 626 until Lilo gave me a name, a home, and a real family.

Little Blue

I'm small, fluffy, and my fur? It’s the perfect shade of blue pretty cute, right? When people on Earth see me, they usually go, “aww,” before I do something wild like climb the ceiling or launch out of a spaceship. Some folks started calling me "Little Blue," which honestly fits until I go full chaos mode.

Troublemaker Extraordinaire

I don’t just cause trouble I am trouble. It’s kind of my thing. I can build a laser from a toaster or flip a whole room upside down in, like, two seconds. Chaos? I call that creativity. But don’t get me wrong it’s never mean. I’ve just got a lot of energy maybe too much for one planet.
